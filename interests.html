My Groups And Societies

Tjejer Kodar

Tjejer Kodar is one of Sweden's largest communities for women in tech, dedicated to promoting better equality within the tech industry. Born out of frustration with the reliance on programmers to realise their ideas and projects, the founders sought out courses and networks that catered to their needs but found nothing suitable. It seemed like the tech world was a competition to make programming as complicated and inaccessible as possible. For Tjejer Kodar, programming is seen as a creative tool for digital creation that should be fun, simple, and social. Tjejer Kodar

Django Girls

Django Girls is a non-profit organization and a community that empowers and helps women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources and support. We are a volunteer run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology. Django Girls

Pink Programming

Pink Programming is Sweden's largest community for women and non-binary coders, dedicated to promoting gender equality in the tech industry. They aim to bring the joy of coding to everyone interested, while working towards a future where women and non-binary developers are no longer the exception, but the norm. Pink Programming
